The Residence

Set into the terraced hillside of Green Coast Resort above Palasë, Camille is a one-bedroom residence on the fourth floor. Full-height glazing opens the living space to the Ionian sea ahead and the Ceraunian mountains rising behind — the Albanian Riviera lived slowly: cypress, stone and sea, moments from the resort's beach, spa and restaurants.

Location & setting

Life at Green Coast

Green Coast is the Albanian Riviera's most established resort address — a stone-built village above the Blue Flag beach at Palasë, where the Ceraunian mountains meet the Ionian. Mediterranean architecture in local stone, travertine and timber; promenades, pools, restaurants and a beach club below; the life of the coast a few steps from the door.
